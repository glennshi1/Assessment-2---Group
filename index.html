Project Idea

Overview

Our project idea is to develop a game using the game engine Unity. The project will be a platform type game, or platformer. Acknowledging that making a complete game could take an extraordinary amount of time, effort, and money, which would be unfeasible for the scope of this project, we have decided to produce a demo version of the game. By completing this project, we hope to gain some of the experience, knowledge, and skills required for our dream jobs.


Motivation

This project has the realistic goals of being interesting and entertaining for the user. Though notably, a future expansion to develop the game fully could see it entering the gaming market, one of the fastest growing sectors of present. The global gaming market was valued at USD 162.32 billion in 2020 and is expected to reach a value of USD 295.63 billion by 2026. While there is a massive market for gaming, it is also highly competitive and dominated by big names and companies.


Description

The project will be a platform, action, side-scroller game. The overall setting and theme of the game and its elements will be based on a story; however, the story will have little importance in the actual gameplay which will be the primary focus. As this is my first time creating such a project, there will almost certainly be some alterations and changes to some of the described features and components at a future date.


The main objective for the player will be to progress, moving across from left to right, where there will be an end goal. The player will have to move and jump across platforms and figure out their way past obstacles and enemies. The player will have movement such as left, right, and jump. Movement will come from arrow key inputs from the user. The player will also have an attack function to use against enemies which will likely come from the spacebar key.

There will be sound effects in the game, based on certain actions, events and interactions with the environment.

Ideally, the game would also include an original soundtrack or OST. However, since our team does not have experience in creating music, this will most likely require outsourcing and is such something to consider after the demo version is released.

The game will also have objects, sprites and backgrounds. These would preferably be original, but as technical art and drawing is not in our team’s skill set, it will also require outsourcing.

We acknowledge that having original art and music will be crucial in making the game as unique and marketable as possible, especially considering that there are an endless number of games already published. However, considering the time and budget constraints, these will be future developments.

Initially, the game will be made for PC, with the possibility of a mobile version being made later. Eventually the game will be released through Steam, a video game digital distribution service where it can reach a wide audience and be distributed globally.


Tools and Technologies

In order to develop this project, we need both hardware and software. The essential hardware required is a PC and its peripherals - a monitor, mouse, keyboard, etc. Alternatively, a laptop or something similar that can run the required software will also be sufficient. The required software includes a game engine such as Unity or Unreal, a digital audio workstation such as Studio One, and digital art software like Adobe Photoshop. Access to the internet and communication tools with team members is also essential.


Skills Required

We have four major skill sets we required for this project: management, programming, art, and music. Project management is an important aspect of this project as someone will be required to lead the team in order to keep us on track and to meet our goals before the deadline. Programming and experience creating a game with a game engine is another important skill for this project, as it will reduce the amount of time required to develop the game itself. We will also require an artist to create the sprites, backgrounds and digital art in general. Finally, we will require someone proficient in using digital audio workstation (DAW) to create the audio and soundtracks for this project. Acquiring these various skills should be feasible, if not for our demo then for our full version.


Outcome

The project’s goal is simply to provide entertainment and enjoyment for the user. Although, making a profit by selling the game is not outside the realm of possibility, it is also not a core aim for us. We are not motivated by money, but by developing ourselves as IT professionals. This project will help us develop our knowledge and understanding of game development, programming, collaboration, and time management, and as such, we are thankful for being given the opportunity to work on it. In the end, we will consider our project a success if our users enjoy playing our game.
